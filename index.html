Peach Cobbler

  • Prep:20 min
  • Cook:40 min
  • Servings:4

    • Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) cans or 8 pitted peaches cut into wedges
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teasopoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, chilled and cut into cubes
